How to train a bird on you?

As I said before, birds can be handfed or not handfed. Handfed means they are trained for humans, while others cannot. So you can buy birds that are handfed, but they cost too much. But you can also train the birds that are not handfed on you. On the first and second day, leave them alone, whether it's handfed or not. Then you start sitting beside it calmly, don't scare it, and don't get a lot of people beside it, only one. Then after 3 weeks, you can start opening the door of the cage and put the best food for birds far away from it while you holding it. It will start eating it, but make sure to not get your hands close to it. Also make sure to make only one person to train it, not a couple, after 6 months, you can start making other people training it. You start getting your hands closer and closer and then after 2-3 months, they will start standing on you hands. After that, start training them for a couple of more months until when they are used to you, you can take them out of their cage. Make sure to put things in your house for them to stand on, so if they fly, they can stand on something. And then you start training it on other people.🧑‍🤝‍🧑🧑🏽‍🤝‍👨
